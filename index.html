Lily's Cafe & Restaurant

Our small business client was delighted to put his menu and restaurant up on the web! Prior to collaborating with HummingCode, they had no idea how to start with attaining a website but knew they wanted something clean. Tea & Coffee

Coffee shops have been some of our favorite work settings, yet some have been hard to find because they are not on the web or have very little description of what they offer. We decided to make a mock coffee website of what would be a great website that would prompt us to visit and stay. This tea & coffee website tells the background story, provides images with a description of their best dishes, and provides the menu! All for customers to have a taste of what to expect.
